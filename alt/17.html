Install Acrylic Panels
Mounting acrylic side and top panels

Install the acrylic side panels by inserting them into the side panel grooves and securing them with 4x M3x8 screws into the top middle frame heatsets (2 on each side).

Install the acrylic top panel by inserting it into the top panel grooves. You may need to adjust the front middle panel position to get the acrylic in easier.
