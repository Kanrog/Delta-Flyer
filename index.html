Boldly print where no filament has gone before! This isn't just any 3D printer ‚Äî it's the Delta Flyer, a marvel of modern engineering that would make even Starfleet engineers jealous. Whether you're replicating spare parts for your own starship or just printing the perfect coffee cup, this delta-configuration beauty is ready for warp-speed printing.

The Delta Flyer is a collaborative project designed by Rolohaun and Kanrog, with a building kit made by LDO. This high-performance delta 3D printer combines innovative design with practical functionality to deliver exceptional print quality and speed.

Ready to build your own? Head over to the Printables page for the STL files and come back here for the assembly instructions. Then have a look below for the essential configuration files to get your Delta Flyer operational!

Want a fun and easy way to decide on what colors to use for your build? get Rolohaun's ColorCAD tool!
